figures vary slightly (±0.5 yr) between sources depending on methodology and year;
values used here are rounded and cross-checked across the references below.

## Notes on accuracy
- "Overall 84.8 years" is the population average; women (87.1) and men (81.1) are reported separately by MHLW.
- The "~5 year gap vs the US" derives from comparing Japan (~84.8) and the US (~80.0).
- Obesity rate (~4.3%) is an OECD-style adult obesity figure; methodology differs from US/EU surveys, so direct comparison is approximate.
